background

High School

My design journey started during the first year of high school when I was introduced to web development for the very first time.
Right away I was fascinated by the endless possibilites and the intuitive design process, but I didn't really think much of it.

Abroad Experience

I had the chance to spend my fourth year of high school abroad, in Liverpool, UK. This was the time when my passion for web design and development truly sparked.
I have always been a perfectionist when it comes to colors, shapes and layout but at this point in time I finally had a way to express it. Experimenting with a lot of different styles allowed me to find my own.

University

I am currently studying as a first year Software Engineering student, at the University of Trento. It has so far been an extremely fruitful experience, and I got the chance to meet some intelligent and talented individuals.
Alongside my studies I have kept alive my passion for web design, improving exponentially and becoming mostly self-taught.

As a now seasoned developer, I believe that there is much more to a website than the mere design; every website is an experience for the user, and I aim to make it as smooth and engaging as possible.

Skillset

With a diverse background in design, I bring a range of skills to the table. I'm confident in taking on any project that comes my way, big or small. My goal is to deliver top-notch results that meet and surpass your expectations.
